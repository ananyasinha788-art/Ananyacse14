Breaking News: AI Revolution in Education

Published on: 13 October 2025

New Delhi: The Ministry of Education has announced a new initiative that integrates Artificial Intelligence into the school curriculum.

The move aims to enhance learning experiences and prepare students for the rapidly evolving job market.

“AI will not replace teachers, but empower them to teach better,” said the Education Minister.

Experts believe that this step will make India a global leader in AI-driven education.

Source: The National Times
